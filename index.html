About

A generalist's range, a craftsman's care.

Over the last decade and a half I've worked across the full spectrum of consumer software — the phone in your pocket, the browser on your desk, and the television in your living room. That breadth has taught me how to move fluidly between platforms while keeping one thing constant: a relentless focus on the experience.

I care about the parts users never see — clean architecture, thoughtful state management, performance — because those are exactly what make the parts they do see feel effortless. Whether it's a smooth 60fps animation on mobile or a snappy 10-foot interface navigated by remote, the goal is the same: software that just works.

Today I serve as Director of Technology at Mercury Intermedia, where I lead engineering while staying hands-on across the full stack. A graduate of Middle Tennessee State University and based near Nashville, I enjoy the whole journey — from a rough concept to a shipped product — and I'm just as comfortable prototyping something new as I am hardening and scaling something mature.
