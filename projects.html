Native App · macOS

Baton macOS

A native macOS audio-routing app for complex, multi-device setups. Route any app's audio to any output — all-to-one, one-to-many, per-app splits, even individual channels — with per-app, per-connection, and per-channel volume. Built on Core Audio process taps, so there's no driver, no kernel extension, and no reboot. Drag-and-drop routing board plus a menu-bar extra for quick control.

  • Swift
  • SwiftUI
  • Core Audio
  • Process Taps
  • Realtime IO
  • Menu-bar app
